Linking on anno.wiki

Linking on anno.wiki is not difficult, but it requires that you know exactly how to do it.

All internal anno.wiki linking should utilize the double bracket style of Wikipedia.

  • To link a writer: [[Writer:Leo Tolstoy]]
  • To link a text: [[Text:War and Peace]].
  • To link an edition: [[Text:War and Peace:1]] where 1 is the edition number.
  • To link specific lines: [[Text:War and Peace:l2-3]]. Without specifying the edition the default edition will be selected.
  • To link specific lines in a specific edition: [[Text:War and Peace:1:l2-3]]
